How it started

The Snug Spot began in a home in South West London with a simple frustration: why is it so hard to find beautiful home goods that are actually good for the planet?

We'd spent years watching cheap, disposable homeware pile up — products that looked nice for a season and fell apart by the next. Working on something better on the side, we spent evenings researching suppliers, testing products at home, and figuring out what quality really means beyond a price tag.

In 2026, The Snug Spot launched from our kitchen table on Sydney Road with a small collection and a belief that British homes deserved better.

"I wanted to build something that made people feel good twice — once when they unwrapped it, and once knowing where it came from."

The mission

We source every product with three questions: Is it made well? Is it made fairly? And will it last? If something doesn't pass all three, it doesn't make the cut — no matter how beautiful it looks.

We work hard to find suppliers who share our standards for quality and responsible production. We're always looking for ways to improve our sourcing practices and be more transparent about where our products come from.

We're not perfect — no young company is — but we're committed to doing better every year, and we're honest about where we are on that journey.

What we stand for

Longevity over novelty

We stock things that last years, not seasons. Quality is our first filter — always.

Transparency

We publish where our products come from. Our sourcing page is always open.

Fair trade

Every maker we work with is paid fairly. We believe beautiful things should be made with dignity.

Less but better

We'd rather have twenty things we're proud of than two hundred things we're not.
